Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and pricing.

What's included in your website maintenance service?

Our comprehensive maintenance service includes regular security updates, performance optimization, content updates, backup management, uptime monitoring, and technical support. We ensure your website remains secure, fast, and functional at all times, with detailed monthly reports on performance and improvements.

How long does a typical web development project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A standard website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while complex web applications can take 8-12 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase and maintain transparent communication throughout the project with regular progress updates.

Do you offer custom solutions outside your standard packages?

Absolutely. We specialize in custom digital solutions tailored to your specific business needs. Whether you need a unique web application, specialized integration, or a completely custom digital strategy, our team can design and implement a solution that aligns with your goals and budget.
