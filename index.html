Frequently Asked Questions

If you are wondering how this works in practice, these are some of the questions that come up most often.

How long does a typical website project take?

A standard website project usually takes 46 weeks from kickoff to launch, depending on how many pages you need and how quickly content is provided. More complex builds or projects that require new copy and visuals can take a bit longer. We outline a timeline clearly before we start.

Do you only work with businesses in Windsor?

No. We are based in Windsor, Ontario and understand the local market well, but we also work remotely with service businesses across Canada and beyond. Most projects are done fully online, with calls and screen shares where needed.

What if I already have a website?

Thats common. In many cases we can audit what you already have, keep whats working, and redesign or restructure what isnt. Sometimes it makes sense to rebuild; other times a focused refresh and SEO cleanup is enough. Well give you a straightforward recommendation.
