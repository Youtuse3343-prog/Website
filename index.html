Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services and process

What's included in your website maintenance service?

Our maintenance service includes regular security updates, performance optimization, content updates, backup management, uptime monitoring, and technical support. We ensure your website remains secure, fast, and functional at all times.

How do you measure social media success?

We track KPIs including engagement rate, follower growth, website traffic from social, lead generation, and conversion rates. Monthly reports provide insights and recommendations for optimization.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on project complexity. A standard website takes 4-6 weeks, while complex applications can take 8-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the discovery phase.
